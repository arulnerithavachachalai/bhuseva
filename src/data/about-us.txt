1. Preserve and promote Native culture and traditions across Nations
2. Provide food, shelter, and other essential facilities to spiritually oriented poor and aged persons, sadhus, and orphans, irrespective of caste, creed, sex, or religion
3. Render noble social services for the welfare, development, and peaceful and prosperous life of all beings in the world on a humanitarian basis
4. Provide shelter and assistance during natural calamities and disaster situations
5. Conduct free Yoga camps, Meditation camps, Health camps, Ayurvedic, Siddha, and Naturopathic camps
6. Start and run orphanages for destitute children, including those of widows and those affected by natural calamities
7. Start and run old-age homes for destitute men and women
8. Promote a clean environment and pollution control within operational regions
9. Raise environmental awareness for the protection and development of natural resources
10. Raise nurseries to produce saplings for distribution on a non-profit basis and to sustain and develop forests
11. Promote awareness about global warming, climate change, and environmental pollution, and encourage the use of renewable resources
12. Promote the principle of universal compassion for all living beings by encouraging vegetarianism
13. Establish, maintain, and promote native animal care centers across Nations
14. Build and operate cow care centers (Gosalas) to sustain, protect, and breed native cows
15. Conduct awareness programs for farmers on effective and natural methods of farming
16. Set up free marketing and sales platforms for rural farmers to market and sell their products effectively
17. Establish and promote effective storage facilities for farm products so farmers can safely store them and benefit during adverse conditions
18. Promote Siddha, Naturopathic, Ayurvedic, and herbal systems of healing by conducting free medical camps, Yoga camps, and Meditation camps
19. Promote ancient meditation techniques using modern equipment and contemporary teaching methods
20. Establish and operate Meditation centers across Nations
21. Set up and maintain residential meditation centers for facilitating long-term sadhana
22. Establish educational institutions offering programs in spirituality, philosophy, Yoga, Meditation, and personal development
23. Produce, publish, sell, or distribute books, periodicals, leaflets, brochures, and papers, and establish and maintain libraries and reading rooms
24. Function as a non-communal and secular organization
25. Acquire and maintain movable and immovable properties required to achieve the objectives of the Trust
